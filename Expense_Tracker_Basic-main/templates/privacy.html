Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided at registration
  • Password — stored as a secure hash; we never store your plain-text password
  • Expense records — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the Service

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to provide and improve the Spendly experience:

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns
  • To allow you to filter, edit, and manage your records
  • To send you important service-related communications (e.g., password resets)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the Service. We do not sell your data.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secure database on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access, including password hashing and access controls.

You can delete individual expense records at any time from within the app. To request full account deletion and removal of all associated data, contact us at the address below.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to deliver the app:

  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log your IP address when fonts are fetched. See Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate with analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media tracking. Your expense data never leaves our servers to any third party.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing at any time by discontinuing use of the Service

To exercise any of these rights, please reach out to us using the contact details below.

Questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data? Contact us at privacy@spendly.com and we'll respond within a few business days.
